Short Bio:


Hello, my name is Marina and I am originally from Chicago. I grew up in a diverse city and made lifelong friends in high school. I attended school in Illinois until I graduated with my bachelor's degree and moved to Seattle for graduate school. Though English is my native language, I am proficient in Lao, Thai, and Spanish. I am a certified teacher in Washington state and spent a few years as a math and science teacher at Ballard High School, where I also founded their competitive Dance Team program. I have spent the last quarter of my life as a caregiver to various friends and family members and lent support where needed. My passions are dance, music, and animals. I especially love dogs and salsa dancing.
